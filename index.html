Heat Bulbs

Global warming is not just about temperatures rising : it's also about acceleration; how would you really see it ?
Here, each country is represented by a hexagonal bulb, from 1743 to 2013; each year the country break his own record of heat (the mean of the year), the bulb is turned on and then turns off gradually.
You can also click on a bulb, or search a country to see the evolution of his temperatures through the years.
Now, let's see how the bulbs are more and more turned on recently !
